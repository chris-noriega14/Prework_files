Bio

    Creedence Clearwater Revival (also known as CCR) was an American rock band formed in California in 1959. CCR was formed by lead vocalist John Fogerty, rhythm guitarist Tom Fogerty, bassist Stu Cook, and drummer Doug Clifford.
    CCR recorded and performed under various names until settling on the name in 1967. Their musical genres included roots rock, swamp rock, blues rock, and Southern rock.
